## 2026-02-21T11:30:00+01:00 · Warsaw, PL

- Target audience: founders & aspiring entrepreneurs (day one), dev teams (later)
- Brand personality: warm & confident (Notion meets Stripe)

- Symbol direction: abstract rooster across 4 conceptual territories

- 100-point evaluation framework defined (5 categories: Visual Design, Metaphor, Color, Typography-Icon Harmony, Audience Resonance)
- Brand Foundation document published as self-contained HTML at brand.kurnik.ai/brand-foundation.html
